The Wonders Beneath the Waves

The waters that border Unawatuna’s beaches harbour an amazing variety of natural beauty and biodiversity – the best way to get up-close and experience all this wonder is to dive right in! Diving in Unawatuna is one of the best things to do here in Sri Lanka’s South Coast, so don’t pass up on the opportunity during your stay here at the Araliya Beach Resort and Spa.

Ride the Waves!

Having some of the best beaches found in Sri Lanka, Unawatuna is the perfect location to be at if you’re in the mood for some great surfing action. There’s plenty of surfing spots to choose from in Unawatuna and most them are just a quick drive away from our resort, the Araliya Beach Resort and Spa. The deeper you go, the more challenging the waves get – be sure to get some experience under your belt first.
